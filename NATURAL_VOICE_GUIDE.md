## üé≠ Why Some Voices Sound Robotic

**Built-in browser voices use two technologies:**

### 1. **Concatenative Synthesis** (Older, Robotic)
- Stitches together pre-recorded sound clips
- **Sounds:** Choppy, robotic, monotone
- **Examples:** Most default system voices

### 2. **Neural/AI Synthesis** (Newer, Natural)
- Uses deep learning to generate speech
- **Sounds:** Smooth, expressive, human-like
- **Examples:** 
  - iOS: "Enhanced" voices
  - Windows: "Natural" or "Online" voices
  - Google: "Neural" voices

**To get neural voices:**
- iOS: Download "Enhanced Quality" voices in Settings
- Windows: Use voices with "(Natural)" in the name
- Android: Google voices are usually neural
